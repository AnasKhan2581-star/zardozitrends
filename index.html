2026 Trends: Fabrics, Colors & Collections

Trendy Fabrics

1. Raw Silk

Premium 80g raw silk holding deep dyes perfectly. A stable canvas for heavy zardozi, dense dabka, and tilla work.

2. Organza

Crisp and translucent for pastel double-dupattas and flowing gowns. Perfect for silver embellishments.

3. Premium Velvet

Micro-velvet in deep crimsons and moody maroons, heavily adorned with antique gold for a regal Barat aura.

4. Tissue & Maisuri

Metallic shimmer fabrics making a massive comeback, elevating intricate threadwork to new heights.
